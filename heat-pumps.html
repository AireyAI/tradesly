What we cover

Every kind of heat pump install.

Whatever your property, our matched installers can scope, size, and quote.

Air source heat pumps

The most common choice for UK homes. Fits outside on a small footprint, no excavation needed. Most efficient when paired with a well-insulated property.

Ground source heat pumps

Higher upfront cost, lower running cost long-term. Requires garden space for trenches or boreholes. Best for larger properties or new-builds.

Hybrid systems

Heat pump paired with your existing gas or LPG boiler. Switches automatically based on outside temperature. Lower install cost, partial grant eligibility.

FAQ

Heat pump questions, answered.

How much does a heat pump cost in the UK?
Air source systems typically run £8,000–£14,000 installed before grant. With the £7,500 Boiler Upgrade Scheme grant, your out-of-pocket can drop to £500–£6,500. Ground source is higher (£18,000–£28,000 before grant) but qualifies for the same grant. Tradesly is free for homeowners — installers pay us a small matching fee per quote request, never you. Your matched installers will give you a precise quote based on your property.
How does the £7,500 BUS grant work?
The Boiler Upgrade Scheme is a UK government grant. Your installer applies on your behalf and the grant is deducted directly from your invoice — you don't pay upfront and claim back. Eligibility requires owning the property, having an EPC, and having any loft/cavity insulation recommendations addressed. Our matched installers handle the entire grant process.
How long does an install take?
Most air source installs take 2–5 days on-site. Ground source takes longer due to trenching/borehole work — typically 1–3 weeks. From quote acceptance to install date, expect 4–10 weeks depending on installer schedules.
Will a heat pump work in my old house? Are installers vetted?
Yes — but the install needs more thought. Older homes often need additional insulation, larger radiators, or in some cases underfloor heating to perform efficiently. A good installer will spec the system to your property's heat-loss profile. Every installer on Tradesly is MCS-certified (a hard regulatory requirement to apply the BUS grant), insured, and held to a quality bar. Installers who let customers down are paused automatically.
