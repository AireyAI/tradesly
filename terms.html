1. What Tradesly is (and isn't)

Tradesly is a quote-matching service. We introduce homeowners to up to three vetted local tradespeople in their postcode for a specific trade.

We are not:

  • A contractor or tradesperson — we don't do any work ourselves
  • An employer of the tradespeople — they are independent businesses
  • A guarantor of work quality — see Section 5
  • A directory or listing site — every tradesperson is vetted before joining

2. Eligibility (homeowners)

To use Tradesly as a homeowner you must be:

  • At least 18 years old
  • The owner or authorised occupier of the UK property the work is for
  • Authorised to share the property and contact details you submit

3. Service is free for homeowners

Tradesly is and always will be free for homeowners. We do not charge you to request quotes, receive matches, or use any feature of the service. Tradespeople pay us a small matching fee for receiving your details — this never affects what they charge you.

4. The matching process

When you submit a quote request:

  • We match you with up to three vetted local tradespeople covering your postcode
  • We share your contact details and problem description with the matched tradespeople
  • The tradespeople contact you directly to provide quotes
  • You compare quotes and choose who (if anyone) you want to hire
  • You contract directly with the tradesperson — Tradesly is not a party to that contract

5. Quality, liability & disputes (homeowners)

We vet every tradesperson before they join Tradesly (insurance, certifications, references, quality bar). However:

  • Disputes about the work itself are between you and the tradesperson

7. Tradesperson Partner Terms — eligibility

To join Tradesly as a tradesperson partner you must:

  • Operate a UK-registered business (sole trader, partnership, or limited company)
  • Hold all required certifications for your trade (e.g. Gas Safe for gas work, MCS for heat pumps and solar, NICEIC for electrical, CISRS for scaffolding)

We verify these details before accepting partners. Misrepresentation may result in immediate removal and recovery of any leads delivered.

8. How leads are priced & delivered

Leads are graded into four tiers based on qualification depth:

  • Tier 1 (Cold): form submitted, basic details — typically £20–£40
  • Tier 2 (Verified): lead replied to confirmation — typically £40–£80
  • Tier 3 (Quote-ready): timeframe + clarified problem — typically £80–£150
  • Tier 4 (Detailed): photos, property type, or specific date — typically £120–£250

You set which tiers you accept at sign-up. Final tier prices vary by trade and are confirmed in your partner dashboard. Leads are delivered by email and (if you opt in) WhatsApp within minutes of being matched.

9. The 24-hour dispute window

You may dispute any lead within 24 hours of delivery for any of the following reasons:

  • Contact details are fake or unreachable
  • Lead is outside your declared postcode coverage
  • Lead has already engaged a different tradesperson
  • Lead is a duplicate of one already delivered to you
  • Lead is for work you don't cover

Disputed leads are auto-refunded by credit on your next invoice. We don't argue or require evidence beyond the dispute reason — your time is worth more than the lead price. Repeated disputes from the same source flag that source for quality review.

Disputes received after 24 hours are at our discretion.

10. Billing

You authorise us to invoice you weekly via Stripe for leads delivered (less any disputed leads). Invoices are due on receipt. Failed payments may result in suspension after 7 days notice.

Your first 3 qualifying leads in your selected niches and postcodes are free as a trial. Standard pricing applies thereafter.

11. No commission, no exclusivity (unless agreed)

We charge a flat per-lead fee. We do not take a percentage of the work you contract with the homeowner. By default, leads are exclusive to one partner per enquiry. We may offer shared leads (lower price, up to 3 partners per lead) in high-volume niches — you opt in to shared leads in your dashboard.

You can cancel your account entirely at any time. Outstanding invoices remain payable.
