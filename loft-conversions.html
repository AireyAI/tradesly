FAQ

Loft conversion questions, answered.

How much does a loft conversion cost in the UK?
Loft conversions typically run £30,000–£60,000 depending on size and type. Dormer is usually £35–45k for a standard 4×4m room. Mansard runs £50–70k. Hip-to-gable £35–55k. Prices vary by region — London & the South-East trend higher. Tradesly is free for homeowners — specialists pay us a small matching fee per quote request, never you.
Do I need planning permission?
Many loft conversions fall under permitted development (no planning needed) but mansards, conservation areas, and bigger volume increases typically require planning. Your matched specialist will assess your property and handle the application if needed (typically £200–500 fee plus council application cost).
How long does the work take?
Plan on 8–14 weeks from quote acceptance to handover. Roughly: 1–2 weeks for design and structural calculations + 4–6 weeks for the build + 2–3 weeks for finishing (electrics, plastering, decoration). Faster if planning is already in place.
Are Tradesly specialists vetted?
Every loft specialist on Tradesly is insured, building-regs experienced, and held to a quality bar. We pause anyone who lets customers down based on dispute reports. We don't list directories or unverified businesses.
