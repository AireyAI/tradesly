FAQ

Tradespeople questions, answered.

How much do I pay per lead?
£20 to £250 depending on tier (see pricing above). Most tradespeople accept T3 and T4 leads only — these are quote-ready homeowners with details and timeframe. Average ticket: £80–£150. You set which tiers you accept at sign-up and can change anytime.
What if a lead is fake or already sorted?
Reply DISPUTE within 24 hours of delivery with the reason (fake number, already sorted, wrong area, duplicate). We auto-refund. Repeat-offender leads get the source flagged so we don't send similar in future. We don't argue — your time is more valuable than the lead price.
How fast do leads come?
As fast as homeowners ask. Some niches (drains, plumbing emergencies) get multiple leads per week per postcode. Others (heat pumps, loft conversions) are 1–4 leads/week per postcode. We grow gradually — you can always increase your daily cap.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. Pause your account in one click, no notice period. Cancel anytime. We invoice weekly via Stripe for leads delivered — there's nothing to terminate, just stop accepting leads.
Do you take commission on the job?
No. You quote the homeowner, you close the deal, you keep 100% of the job revenue. We take a flat lead fee at the point of delivery — never a percentage of the work you do.
Are leads exclusive to me?
By default leads are sent to ONE buyer per enquiry (exclusive). In high-volume niches and postcodes we may offer shared leads (up to 3 buyers, lower price). You opt in to shared leads at sign-up — by default exclusive only.
