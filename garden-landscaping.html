FAQ

Garden questions, answered.

How much does a garden project cost in the UK?
Garden projects vary hugely. Small fence repair: £200–£600. New decking: £1,500–£5,000. Full garden redesign with patio, fence, and planting: £8,000–£25,000+. Prices vary by region — London & the South-East trend higher. Tradesly is free for homeowners — landscapers pay us a small matching fee per quote, never you.
How long does the work take?
Small jobs (fencing, lawn) usually 1–3 days. Decking 3–7 days. Full design + build 4–12 weeks depending on scale and weather. Spring/summer slots fill fast — book early if you want work done in peak season.
Do I need planning permission?
Most garden work doesn't need planning. Decking over 30cm high might. Walls and fences over 2m high do (1m if next to a road). Trees with TPOs can't be touched without council approval. Your matched landscaper will flag if planning is needed.
Are Tradesly landscapers vetted?
Every landscaper on Tradesly is insured, references-checked, and held to a quality bar. Landscapers who let customers down are paused automatically based on dispute reports. We don't list directories or unverified businesses.
