FAQ

Drains & plumbing questions, answered.

How much does drain unblocking or plumbing cost in the UK?
Drain unblocking £80–£250 typical (more for complex jobs blocked out to the sewer). Plumbing callout £60–£90 plus £40–£70/hour labour. Leak detection £150–£400 depending on complexity. Tradesly is free for homeowners — plumbers pay us a small matching fee per quote.
How fast can someone come out for an emergency?
Most matched plumbers can attend within 2–4 hours for genuine emergencies (burst pipes, total blockages, no hot water in winter). Non-urgent jobs typically next-day or within 48 hours. We prioritise emergency tags in matching.
Are Tradesly plumbers vetted and Gas Safe registered?
Every drainage specialist on Tradesly is insured. All gas and boiler work is Gas Safe registered (a UK legal requirement — we don't list anyone who isn't). Specialists who let customers down are paused automatically based on dispute reports.
Are out-of-hours callouts more expensive?
Emergency callouts outside 9–5 typically attract a 30–50% surcharge. Your matched specialist will confirm pricing before attending so there are no surprises on the invoice. The quote you accept is what you pay.
