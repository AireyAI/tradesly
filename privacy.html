Privacy Policy.

Last updated: 2026-05-13 · UK GDPR compliant

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data Tradesly ("we", "us") collects, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

The short version: We collect the details you submit when requesting a quote (name, postcode, email, phone, problem description). We share them with up to three matched local tradespeople so they can send you quotes. We never sell your data, and we never share it without your explicit consent. You can request deletion at any time by emailing hello@tradesly.co.uk.

1. Who we are

Tradesly is a UK-based quote-matching service operated by AireyAI Limited. We connect homeowners with vetted local tradespeople. We are the data controller for personal data submitted through this website.

Contact: hello@tradesly.co.uk

2. What data we collect

Data you submit voluntarily

  • Name (when provided in the problem field)
  • Postcode (used to match you with local tradespeople)
  • Email address (so matched tradespeople can contact you)
  • Phone number (optional — speeds up callbacks if you provide it)
  • WhatsApp number (optional — alternative to phone)
  • Problem description (so the tradesperson can prepare an accurate quote)
  • Consent timestamp (when you ticked the GDPR consent box)

Data collected automatically

  • IP address (for fraud prevention and rate limiting)
  • Referring page (so we know how you arrived)
  • Browser type and device (anonymised, for service quality)
  • Pages visited (anonymised analytics — no third-party trackers)

3. How we use your data

We use your personal data only to:

  • Match you with up to three vetted local tradespeople in your postcode for the trade you requested
  • Send you a confirmation email asking you to verify your enquiry (so we don't waste tradespeople's time on bot submissions)
  • Pass your details to the matched tradespeople via email (and WhatsApp if you provided that number)
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent submissions
  • Improve our matching algorithm (using anonymised aggregate data only)

Lawful basis: We rely on your explicit consent (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)) given via the consent checkbox on the form, and our legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) in operating the matching service and preventing fraud.

4. Who we share your data with

We share your details with:

  • Up to three matched tradespeople in your postcode for the trade you requested. They are independent businesses contracted to use your details only to provide a quote, not to add you to marketing lists.
  • Email service providers (e.g. Google Workspace, used to send confirmation and matching emails)
  • Hosting providers (Cloudflare and GitHub Pages, who store the encrypted form submission in transit)
  • Anti-fraud providers (basic IP-based checks, no third-party data enrichment)

We never sell your data, share it with marketing companies, or pass it to anyone outside the matching flow described above.

5. How long we keep your data

  • Lead records: kept for 12 months for audit and dispute resolution. After that, personal identifiers are redacted; only anonymised event data is retained for analytics.
  • Consent records: kept indefinitely (legal requirement to prove consent was given).
  • Email correspondence: kept for as long as our email provider retains it (typically 7 years for tax purposes).

You can request earlier deletion at any time — see "Your rights" below.

6. Your rights under UK GDPR

Under UK GDPR you have the following rights at any time:

  • Right of access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — correct any inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time, with no penalty

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@tradesly.co.uk. We respond within 30 days (usually much faster).

7. Cookies

We use a minimal set of essential cookies for session management and basic anonymised analytics. We do not use third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or social media trackers.

8. Children

Tradesly is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a minor has submitted data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International data transfers

Some of our service providers (e.g. email hosting) may process your data outside the UK. Where this happens, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place under UK GDPR Chapter V (Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent adequacy decisions).

10. Complaints

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, please contact us first at hello@tradesly.co.uk — we'll do our best to resolve it.

You also have the right to complain to the UK regulator, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):

  • Website: ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint
  • Phone: 0303 123 1113

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent change. Material changes will be flagged on the homepage.
